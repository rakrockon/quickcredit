The Problem
People need small, short-term money instantly to cater to many temporary situations arising out of day-to-day life. So, what do you do when you need instant money to solve your temporary troubles? How else can you avail cash loans without worrying about your credit reports?
The Solution
In such temporary situations, cash loans are a great alternative to traditional bank loans. QuickCredit.in is here to help you with instant money when you need it the most! We facilitate short-term cash loans in a simple, quick and flexible way.
Application Process
Our application is completely online from start to finish. You need to complete a simple one-time registration process to become an approved borrower. We evaluate you based on your Personal & Professional Credibility and your Social Credibility. Once you register as a borrower, you can avail cash loans instantly, one click, 24*7 and without needing any further verification, documentation, etc...
Borrower Eligibility

To register as a borrower, you must be over 21 years of age and an Indian resident. For your borrower membership to be approved, you would need to provide us the following documents (soft copies):

Proof of ID (POI): Company ID or Pan Card
Proof of Address (POA): Passport or Aadhar Card or Driving
License or Voter ID
Latest Salary Slip
Bank Account Statement for the last six months

Note: These documents are needed only for the one-time registration process
